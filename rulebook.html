General Instructions

  • The participants are requested to bring their college ID card/bonafide.
  • Participants need to fill the Registration link to confirm their participation before 26.02.2025 and to join whatsapp group for further updates.
  • Registration fee for each participant is Rs. 100/- have to be paid via Gpay number: **, Name of the payee: **
  • Each student can register for maximum two events after ensuring that timings doesn't overlap.
  • For group events each student should register individually and one from group can make a single payment.
  • The participants are requested to be present 15 minutes prior to the inaugural function.
  • Confirmation of prior registrations and spot registration for some events will take place between 08.00 am to 09.15 am on 27.12.2023. No further registrations will be encouraged.
  • The participants must reach their respective event venues on time and updates regarding venues will be posted via whatsapp group.
  • Refreshments will be provided.
